🏃 Claude Running Coach

AI-powered training plans and coaching for your next race

Training for a race?

Claude Running Coach is an AI assistant that creates personalized training plans, analyzes your workouts, and provides evidence-based coaching guidance. It works with your existing training data from Strava, Garmin, Apple Health, or manual logs.

Instead of paying for expensive coaching or following generic plans, have a conversation with Claude to get training advice tailored to your goals, schedule, and fitness level.

What You Get

Training Plans

Personalized plans for 5K, 10K, half marathon, and marathon with scientifically-calculated pace zones and progressive weekly schedules.

Workout Analysis

Review your training logs with lap-by-lap breakdowns, compare actual vs. planned workouts, and track your progress.

Weekly Coaching

Get personalized feedback on your training execution, pacing, recovery, and adjustments to keep you on track.

Flexible Data Sources

Works with Strava, Garmin, Apple Health, or manual logs. Even conversational descriptions of your training.

How It Works

Claude Running Coach is a plugin for Claude Code and Claude Desktop that extends Claude's capabilities with running-specific knowledge and tools.

Example Conversation

You: "Create a 12-week half marathon training plan. My race is on March 15th, I'm currently running 25 miles per week, and my goal is to finish in 1:35:00."

Claude: Generates a complete training plan with calculated pace zones, weekly mileage progression, and specific workouts.

You: "Sync my last week of training from Strava"

Claude: Downloads your activities and creates a training log with weekly totals and workout analysis.

You: "Coach me on this week"

Claude: Compares your actual training against the plan, analyzes your performance, and provides coaching feedback.

Setup

You'll need:

  • Claude Code or Claude Desktop - Free AI assistant app
  • Python 3.8+ - For pace calculations (likely already installed)
  • Training data - From Strava, other apps, or manual entry
Note: The Strava integration is optional. You can use Claude Coach with data from any running platform or by manually describing your training.
